Instructions

Choose a square size, then place the numbers in the grid such that:

  • Each number appears exactly once.
  • Every row, column, and main diagonal has the same total.

Drag and drop the numbers to solve the puzzle. Once two or more lines are complete, the puzzle will tell you whether those completed lines agree.

You can drag numbers back to the deck if needed. Changing the square size starts a fresh puzzle.
